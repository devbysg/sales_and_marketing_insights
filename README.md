# Sales & Marketing Performance Dashboard

## Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing sales and market performance using a multi-table dataset
structured in a fact and dimension model. The dashboard provides insights into revenue trends,
sales quantity, market contribution, and top-performing customers and products across multiple years.

## Tools & Technologies
- Tableau
- Excel
- CSV Datasets

## Dataset Description
- transactions.csv: Sales transaction-level data (fact table)
- customers.csv: Customer master data
- products.csv: Product master data
- markets.csv: Market and region details
- date.csv: Date dimension for time-based analysis

## Key KPIs & Analysis
- Total Revenue
- Total Sales Quantity
- Revenue by Market
- Sales Quantity by Market
- Revenue Trend (Year & Month)
- Top 5 Customers by Revenue
- Top 5 Products by Revenue
